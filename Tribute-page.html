Tim Berners-Lee

Timeline of Tim Berners-Lee and his accomplishments:
  • Born: 08 June 1955
  • 1973-1976 - Attended Oxford and made a computer out of an old television.
  • 1978 - Joined D.G Nashed and helped create type-setting software for printers.
  • 1980 - While working as a contractor at CERN proposed a project based on hypertext. Built a protype name ENQUIRE.
  • 1989 - CERN was the largest internet node in Europe. Berners-Lee joined hypertext with the internet.
  • 1990 - Berners-Lee sent his proposal to his manager who accepted. Using similar ideas to ENQUIRE system to create the World Wide Web. Later published the first website describing the project.
  • 1994 - Founded the W3C comprised of various companies that were willing to create standards and recommendations to improve the quality of the Web.
